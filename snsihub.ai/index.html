LANDING ZONE

The Landing Zone at SNS Square Technologies LLP is not a program. It allows companies to work at SNS Square Technologies LLP while waiting to join a program, exploring different program options, or working on early-stage development independently.

Selection is as rigorous as for SNS Square Technologies LLP's in-house programs, and it includes access to all campus resources.

It allows one to connect with 300+ mentors and 50+ Investors.
